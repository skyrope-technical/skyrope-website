Who We Are

Sky Rope Technical Services LLC

Sky Rope Technical Service is a UAE-based company offering work-at-height solutions with the highest level of commitment to safety standards. We pride ourselves on satisfying our valued customers by providing quality workmanship and the safest work methods.

We maintain Commercial, Residential, Hospitality, and High-Rise buildings through Facade Cleaning, Asset Maintenance, Signboard Installation, Lighting works, and bespoke customer requirements. Our team of IRATA-certified rope access technicians brings a vast array of skills and disciplines — enabling us to access the most extreme and challenging locations safely and efficiently.
